Frequently Asked Questions

Is a fireplace sound safe to fall asleep to?expand_more

Yes — the crackle is irregular but soft and low-intensity, so it doesn't startle the sleeping brain. The warm tone is one of the most popular sleep textures online.

Why does a cozy cabin sound so relaxing?expand_more

It triggers a deep "safe shelter" response. Rain outside + fire inside = millennia of human evolution telling the body to settle and rest.

Can I use this for stress and anxiety, not just sleep?expand_more

Absolutely — it's excellent for anxiety relief, journaling, meditation, and slow afternoons. The warm tone calms the sympathetic nervous system within minutes.
