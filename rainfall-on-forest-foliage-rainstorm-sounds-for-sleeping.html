Frequently Asked Questions

Why does rain on leaves sound so calming?expand_more

Leaves diffuse each raindrop, softening sharp transients and leaving a fluffy, breath-like texture the brain reads as completely safe.

Is rain without thunder better for babies?expand_more

Often yes — sudden thunderclaps can startle infants out of light sleep. Pure rain provides continuous masking without peaks.

How does this compare to rain on a window?expand_more

Window rain has sharper tapping high frequencies (more ASMR). Forest rain is softer and more diffuse (more meditative). Both excellent — try both.
