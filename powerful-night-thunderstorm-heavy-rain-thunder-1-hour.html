Frequently Asked Questions

Is 1 hour of rain sounds enough to fall asleep?expand_more

For most people, yes — sleep onset typically happens within 10–20 minutes. If you wake up easily, loop the video or use Rainscape for continuous all-night playback.

Will the thunderclaps wake me up?expand_more

No — the thunder is mostly distant and rolling, blended into the rain. Keep the volume moderate and your brain quickly learns to filter the rumble as part of the safe background.

Is windy rain better than steady rain for sleep?expand_more

Many people prefer windy rain because the gusting adds a slow, breath-like rhythm. Steady rain is more uniform; both work — try this one to see which your brain responds to.
