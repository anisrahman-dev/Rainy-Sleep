Frequently Asked Questions

Do rain sounds really help ADHD?expand_more

Yes. Steady ambient noise like heavy rain on a window gives an ADHD brain a baseline of predictable stimulation, freeing focus. Many people with ADHD report rain ASMR helps both sleep and concentration.

Is 30 minutes long enough to fall asleep?expand_more

For most people, yes. Average sleep onset on a calm ASMR track is 10–20 minutes. If you wake up easily, loop the video or use a 1+ hour version.

What makes rain on a window "ASMR"?expand_more

The high-frequency taps of rain on glass trigger the same tingly relaxation response many people get from whispering or tapping — but without the human voice, so it's safer for sleep.
